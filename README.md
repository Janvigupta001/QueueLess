# 🚀 QueueLess

## 📌 Overview
QueueLess is a UI/UX case study designed in Figma to solve long waiting queues at campus food outlets.

The app helps students:
- View real-time queue status
- Check estimated waiting time
- Compare crowd across different food outlets
- Book a digital queue token
- Save time during breaks

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Students often spend 20–30 minutes waiting in food queues during lunch breaks, causing:

- Time wastage
- Missed classes
- Poor food outlet experience
- Long waiting lines

QueueLess aims to reduce waiting time through smart queue visualization and digital token management.
